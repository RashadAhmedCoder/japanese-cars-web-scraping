By Rashad Ahmed

Did you know that Toyota and Honda collectively made up of approximately a whopping 23% of all cars sold in the United States in 2024? Before the car search even starts, many customers are already decided that their next car is going to be from one of those two powerhouse Japanese brands known for their reliability.

Through the power of web scraping, this site reduces the hassle of your car search by displaying every model the two brands have all in one site.
